Intermediate

In the Indian education system of some Indian states, the Pre-University Course (PUC) or Pre-Degree Course (PDC) is referred to as Intermediate or +2 Course, which is an two-year senior secondary education course that succeeds the tenth grade (known as SSLC or SSC in such states, equivalent to sophomore in the US system) and precedes to the completion of an Senior Secondary Course.

Degree

With a choice of subjects and disciplines that can develop knowledge in a chosen area, a degree can demonstrate a commitment to a particular subject. It can provide improved career options and better earning potential. There are three types of degree course that students can study with us. They span a range of subjects including accountancy, business, healthcare, law, nursing and technology.

Post Graduation

IAn advanced qualification which requires a first degree, usually lasting between 1-2 years to enable learners to become specialists in their area.The term “postgraduate degree” covers everything from postgraduate diplomas to master's degrees to doctoral degrees. Essentially, any degree that requires an undergraduate (bachelor's) degree as an admissions prerequisite can be considered a postgraduate degree.
